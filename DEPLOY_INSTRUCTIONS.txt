DEPLOYMENT INSTRUCTIONS FOR NETLIFY
====================================

The project has been built successfully. The 'dist' folder contains your website files.

TO DEPLOY TO NETLIFY:

Option 1 - Netlify Drop (Easiest):
1. Go to: https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Open your terminal and run: open dist
3. Drag the entire contents of the dist folder to the Netlify Drop page
4. Netlify will deploy your site immediately

Option 2 - Manual Upload:
1. Download this entire project
2. Find the 'dist' folder on your computer
3. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop
4. Drag the dist folder contents to deploy

1. Sign up/login at netlify.com
